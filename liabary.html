Zaheen Library

To acknowledge the services, the library is dedicated to Zaheen Ahmed who devoted his entire working life to the cause of Art Education. A graduate of the KSA in 1974, Zaheen remained at the school as a dedicated teacher for the rest of his life. He continues to be sadly missed by faculty and students alike.

The KSA library has a rare collection of books related to different fields of Art and Design. You may find a colossal variety of books covering numerous aspects of the creative industry. The library has over 3000 books, including many rare, original editions. It's primarily for reference, though some books are available for issue.

The library also maintains an Audio/Visual section with documentaries, movies, e-books, and other media related to art and design.

Each student receives a library card at the start of the academic year, renewable annually. All books are catalogued for prompt access.

The Library is primarily for KSA students, faculty, and staff. Visitors from other institutions may be allowed with written permission from the Librarian.
