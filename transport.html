Transport

Karachi School of Art provides a transport facility for both morning and evening students. The fleet of vans covers a wide area of the city, ensuring a comfortable, safe, and punctual commuting experience. Students can avail the transport service at very affordable rates, which are paid directly to the drivers. The administration monitors these services to maintain quality.
